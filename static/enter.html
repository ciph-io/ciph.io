What is Ciph?

Ciph is a premium social video platform that is built on the values of privacy, security and censorship resistance.

Ciph respects copyrights and supports content creators with the best partner program in the industry.

Ciph protects children with a community ratings system and the most advanced parental controls in the industry.

Premium

Ciph is ad free. Ciph users pay for the data they use but everyone gets 10GB free data per month.

Privacy

Ciph does not log or track any user data other than what is needed to maintain a functioning service.

Ciph never records what you watch and only records the amount of data that you use.

Ciph will never release any information regarding your account to any third party unless required by court order.

Security

Ciph uses three layers of encryption to protect data as it is stored and transmitted via the Ciph platform.

Ciph uses industry standard HTTPS encryption to encrypt communications between your browser and our servers and to encrypt communications between our servers.

All data stored and transmitted via Ciph is encrypted using the industry standard AES encryption algorithm.

Finally Ciph applies its proprietary One-Time-Pad (OTP) based cryptography system which makes it impossible to determine which files belong to which content.

Censorship Resistance

Ciph makes censorship impossible without a court order.

The only way Ciph can censor content is by breaking our own encryption system which would be illegal unless we were required to do so by a court order.

Ciph will always comply with lawful orders and assist law enforcement in fighting criminal activity but we will never participate in illegal censorship or surveillance.

Partner Program

Ciph shares 50% of our gross revenue with content creators and promoters.

Because Ciph is an ad-free premium video platform our partner program rewards creators who produce quality content that viewers want to pay for.

"Demonetization" is not a word in our vocabulary. The only way that content creators will not get paid is if they are engaged in illegal activity and Ciph is legally required to suspend them.

Protecting Children

Ciph does not engage in censorship but we do provide parents with tools to protect their children from harmful content.

Ciph's community rating system allows content to be rated by the community and Ciph's parental controls allow parents to restrict what their children can access based on community ratings.

Content that does not have a community rating or has a community rating of "Adults Only" is not accessible to children.
